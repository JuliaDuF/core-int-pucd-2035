CORE 1 INTERACTION D FA20

I'm a student at Parsons School of Design and I'm majoring in Communication Design. My family loves to travel during the vacations so I've got the chance of visiting many countires since I was a child. I love collecting key chains through my trips and I made a board of collection in my room. Throughout these trips I was fascinated by the differences in culture and art in all different parts of the world, which motivated me to study abroad after middleschool. I still keep the habit of buying keychains today when I go to a city or a musuem that I've never been to. Looking at the collection board helps me to bring up the memories that I had during those trips and thus giving me insiprations for future designs.
